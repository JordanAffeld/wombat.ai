Our Founder

Jordan Affeld

Founder & CEO

Jordan Affeld combines deep expertise in cross-cultural business strategy with a passion for helping innovative APAC companies succeed in the U.S. market. With over 10 years of international business development experience in technology, consumer goods, and retail, he has uniquely positioned Wombat as the go-to agency for bridging the East-West divide.

Prior to founding Wombat, Jordan led international expansion for several high-growth tech companies and served as an advisor to multinational corporations on their Asia-Pacific strategies. He is fluent in Mandarin Chinese and French.
